Who We Are?

Adventure Mission Nepal is environment friendly adventure company operating trips in the Himalayas of Nepal, India, Tibet and Bhutan. Most of the trips from AMN are designed to cater the needs of adventure lovers. We operate trips that have mix of cultural and natural beauty as well as the warm hospitality of the people living in the Himalayas. Besides the Adventure trips such as trekking, Peak Climbing and Mountaineering in the Himalayan countries AMN also organizes cultural tours of Monasteries and many other special interest tours.

AMN has a team of efficient and skilled team members along with the best equipments required to navigate through the highest terrain in the world. Besides the excellent customer services we provide our staffs are also well taken care and are provided timely training and refreshment, which has helped us to provide the service par excellence to our valued guests. We have trained our staff in the best possible way for operating programs based on the principles of eco-tourism. We also encourage our clients to help these beautiful Himalayan countries beautiful and undisturbed for the best interest of the future generations.
